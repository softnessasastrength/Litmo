# Litmo Founder Notes

This is the living record of product insights, unresolved questions, phrases, and observations that may shape Litmo.

Founder notes are not automatically specifications. They preserve reasoning so ideas can later be tested, refined, rejected, or promoted into product requirements.

## How to Add a Note

Use this format:

```md
## YYYY-MM-DD — Short title

**Observation**
What happened, what was noticed, or what idea surfaced.

**Possible product implication**
What this might mean for Litmo.

**Status**
Raw thought | Needs research | Candidate principle | Accepted decision | Rejected
```

## Current Notes

## 2026-07-11 — Touch is a language

**Observation**
Touch carries meaning through pressure, duration, placement, timing, intention, and response.

**Possible product implication**
Litmo should help people describe and negotiate touch without pretending a profile can fully capture live human nuance.

**Status**
Accepted principle

## 2026-07-11 — The quiz should feel like BuzzFeed

**Observation**
Clinical questionnaires create distance. Playful, indirect questions can help people recognize preferences without feeling examined.

**Possible product implication**
The vibe quiz should feel whimsical, visual, and emotionally intuitive while remaining transparent about what it is doing.

**Status**
Accepted direction

## 2026-07-11 — Lived experience is evidence, not specification

**Observation**
The founder learned touch through body signals and lived interaction, but personal experience cannot be universalized into fixed rules.

**Possible product implication**
Features should be customizable, inclusive, and tested with people whose experiences differ from the founder's.

**Status**
Accepted principle

## 2026-07-11 — Profiles should preserve nuance

**Observation**
Turning embodied signals into a quiz risks flattening people.

**Possible product implication**
Profiles must be presented as conversation starters and current tendencies, not diagnoses or permanent identities.

**Status**
Accepted principle

## 2026-07-11 — The idea must stand without the company

**Observation**
The public framework is larger than the Litmo implementation.

**Possible product implication**
Talks and public writing should explain why safe platonic touch needs social infrastructure without becoming product advertisements.

**Status**
Accepted principle
