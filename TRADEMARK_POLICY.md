# Trademark Policy

**This is currently a personal emotional containment system, not a public product.**

**Pillar Fit Check:** serves Authenticity Over Performance (Pillar 6) — the
name "Litmo" and "Softness as a Strength" mean something specific and
personal; this document says what that means for anyone reusing them,
instead of leaving it to be assumed later.

**Status:** A plain-language policy, not a registered-trademark legal
claim. Neither "Litmo" nor "Softness as a Strength" is a registered
trademark as of this writing. This document describes how the founder asks
the names and identity be treated — it does not assert trademark rights
that haven't actually been registered, and it is not legal advice. Follow-up
to ADR 0044.

---

## What this covers

- The name **"Litmo"**.
- The name **"Softness as a Strength"** and the softnessasastrength.com
  domain/identity.
- The "Seven Pillars" framing, the "Cathedral" language, and other
  distinctive naming used throughout this project's docs (e.g. "The
  Cathedral Purge," "The 2/382 Doctrine") when used to imply affiliation
  with, or endorsement by, this specific project or founder.

This does **not** cover the source code itself — that's MPL 2.0, see
`LICENSE` and ADR 0044. You can take the code and do almost anything MPL
2.0 permits with it. This document is only about the names and identity
markers layered on top of that code.

## What's asked of forks and derivatives

`docs/FORK_YOUR_OWN_CATHEDRAL.md` already exists to make forking this
project easy and welcome — that has not changed. If you fork this
repository:

1. **You may keep the code, structure, and architecture** under the terms
   of MPL 2.0, with no restriction beyond the license itself.
2. **Please don't present your fork as "Litmo"** (unqualified) or as
   affiliated with, endorsed by, or maintained by the original founder,
   once you've meaningfully diverged from this repository. Call it your
   own name. `scripts/consecrate-your-cathedral.mjs` exists specifically
   to help you replace the identifying details (starting with the
   partner-name placeholder) as part of making it genuinely yours.
3. **"Softness as a Strength" and the Seven Pillars** are the founder's
   personal framework, published at softnessasastrength.com. Referencing
   or crediting that framework in your fork's docs is fine and welcome;
   presenting your fork as an official extension of that site or its
   author is not.
4. **This is a request, not (currently) a legal demand.** Without a
   registered mark, enforcement options are limited to what unregistered
   "common law" trademark protection and ordinary honesty norms provide.
   This document exists so the ask is at least stated plainly instead of
   silently assumed.

## Why no registration yet

This project is explicitly not a commercial product (see
`NON_MONETIZATION_COVENANT.md`, `CONSTITUTION.md`). Trademark registration
exists to protect commercial goodwill and prevent market confusion; a
personal, non-monetized containment system doesn't currently have the
commercial footprint that registration is meant to protect. If this project
is ever handed to someone else, sold, donated, or inherited — which
`NON_MONETIZATION_COVENANT.md` already restricts in other ways — this
policy's asks travel with it the same way that covenant does.

## What this is not

- Not a registered trademark claim. Nothing here should be read as "™" or
  "®" in a legal sense.
- Not a restriction on the source code license. MPL 2.0 governs the code;
  this document only concerns names and identity.
- Not retroactive against uses that predate this document, and not an
  attempt to control unaffiliated third parties who happen to use ordinary
  words ("litmo," "softness," "strength") outside the context of this
  specific project.
- Not legal advice, and not a substitute for the "qualified legal review"
  ADR 0044 still lists as open follow-up work before any production or
  public-facing distribution.
