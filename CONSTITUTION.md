# Litmo Constitution

## Purpose
Litmo exists to help people communicate and negotiate safe, consensual, platonic physical connection. It is infrastructure for human connection, not a substitute for it.

## Core Values
- Consent before convenience.
- Safety before growth.
- People before metrics.
- Warmth before efficiency.
- Transparency before persuasion.

## Non-Negotiable Principles
1. Consent is explicit, session-specific, and revocable.
2. Boundaries always override compatibility.
3. The product never uses dark patterns.
4. Users are never pressured to stay in a session.
5. Soft Signal must always be immediate and respected.
6. Trust is earned through behavior, not gamified.

## Product Philosophy
The app should feel calm, welcoming, and human. It should resemble a thoughtfully designed journal or personality experience rather than a dating app or enterprise tool.

## Design Philosophy
People are more nuanced than profiles. Profiles begin conversations; they do not define people.

## Engineering Philosophy
Readable code, documented decisions, accessibility by default, and privacy-first architecture.

## Decision Rule
When two choices conflict, choose the one that better protects user dignity, consent, and long-term trust, even if it slows development.
