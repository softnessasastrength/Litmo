# Burn Protocol

**THIS IS A PRIVATE EXORCISM ARTIFACT, NOT A PRODUCT.**

Burn = graduation. Not failure. Not giving up. **Release of the obligation to keep perfecting the cage.**

---

## What “burn” means

Any of:

| Form | Action |
| ---- | ------ |
| **Archive** | Freeze repo; no more commits except archival note |
| **Export + delete** | Local dump, then wipe remotes/local as you choose |
| **Literal fire** | Print manifesto + trauma architecture; burn paper |
| **Walk away** | Stop opening the repo; identity does not require it |
| **Soft burn** | Mark `BURNED.md` at root; leave code as museum |

You do not need all forms. One honest form is enough.

---

## Preconditions (from LOGICAL_EXTREME)

Check against [`LOGICAL_EXTREME.md`](LOGICAL_EXTREME.md) gates G1–G6.

If gates fail, you may still burn — but write one page: *why I’m burning incomplete*. That is also honesty.

---

## Ritual steps (suggested)

1. **Read** `EXORCISM_MANIFESTO.md` out loud or slow.  
2. **Read** inventory D01–D24 in `LOGICAL_EXTREME.md`.  
3. **Write** (optional) `docs/BURN_NOTE.md`: date, which form of burn, one sentence of what you keep in the body.  
4. **Choose** form of burn.  
5. **Execute** (archive / delete / fire / walk).  
6. **Do not** immediately start Litmo 2.0 the next morning unless you *choose* a new sacred purpose in writing.

## What you keep after burn

Not the cage.

- The *seeing*  
- Soft Signal as a life skill (leave without fawning)  
- Consent as language (ask, specific, revocable)  
- Softness as non-weak  

The repo was scaffolding. Scaffolding comes down. The building of the self remains.

## What agents must not do

- Rebuild the same defenses under a new name without naming them  
- Treat burn as a bug
