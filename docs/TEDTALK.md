# What Your Nervous System Is Starving For

**Speaker:** Branden | **Runtime:** ~15-17 minutes

> _"Touch is not a transaction. It is a language."_
>
> ---
>
> ## Talk Structure
>
> 1. **The Hook** - Ask the audience to recall the last time they were truly held. Let the silence sit.
> 2. 2. **The Problem** - Touch starvation as a public health crisis. The Surgeon General 2023 loneliness declaration. Harlow wire monkey experiments. Oxytocin, cortisol, vagus nerve science.
>    3. 3. **The Complication** - Why we cannot just go get a hug. Culture conflates touch with sex. Trauma, neurodivergence, missing infrastructure for platonic need.
>       4. 4. **The Insight** - Touch is a language with grammar: pressure, duration, intention. Mutual understanding, not assumption.
>          5. 5. **The Solution** - Litmo: Touch Language Profile, Consent Snapshot, Soft Signal, Trust Ledger.
>             6. 6. **Who This Is For** - Veterans, autistic adults, widows, college students, trauma survivors. All of us.
>                7. 7. **The Vision** - We are not trying to optimize touch. We are trying to protect it.
>                   8. 8. **The Close** - Return to the opening. Land the final line.
