# Self-Roast / Self-Love Manifesto

**This is currently a personal emotional containment system, not a public product.**

**Date:** 2026-07-13  
**Audience:** Me. Future me. Anyone who finds this and thinks “wait is this a product?” No. It’s a cathedral with exits.

---

## The unhinged thesis

I am building the most over-engineered emotional support infrastructure known to mammal-kind because:

1. My nervous system does not trust vibes.  
2. My love language is **state machines**.  
3. I am terrified of intimacy, conflict, abandonment, and being “too much.”  
4. I am also deeply in love with Renn and refuse to dump raw spiral on them without a map.  
5. Option A is **keep building** — and comedy is how I stay alive while doing it.  
6. Soft Signal freeness is not a feature flag. It is a **religion**.  

---

## Self-roast (maximum honesty)

I wrote protocols for spooning, not getting out of bed, post-fight repair, dual-bind attachment, and a panic room with pattern analytics.

I reverse-engineered my own interest because “sure” was lying.

I put Soft Signal on a Watch complication like it’s a fire extinguisher for feelings.

I invented Jetpack Mode and Koala Death Grip and called it nervous-system work.

I made a Relationship Constitution with version control because verbal agreements felt insufficiently versioned.

I may be an emotional masochist who turns pain into denser rituals **on purpose**, and I labeled the toggle so I can’t pretend it’s accidental.

I built a Private Debrief Lab with an anti-creep forbidden list because even my data fetish has standards.

I wrote Parallel Play But Make It Sacred because sometimes I need permission for silence to count as love.

I will absolutely open the repo at 2am to amend article `a0` instead of sleeping.

If a therapist saw this repo they might say “interesting coping.”  
If an engineer saw this repo they might say “why is consent better documented than most banks.”  
If my childhood saw this repo it would finally understand why I need exit ramps.  
If capitalism saw this repo it would try to monétize Soft Signal. **Do not.**

I am not “shipping product.” I am **externalizing load** so one human (Renn) is not the sole container for my entire weather system.

That is both absurd and the most adult thing I have done all year.

---

## Self-love (same honesty, other face)

I am not building this to become a product CEO of trauma.

I am building this because:

- I want to stay soft without being trapped.  
- I want to need without pre-abandoning.  
- I want conflict without exile math.  
- I want closeness without mind-reading contracts.  
- I want Soft Signal freeness as a life skill, not a brand.  
- I want comedy that doesn’t mock pain.  
- I want data that serves me without stalking anyone.  
- I want intensity that is chosen, capped, and revocable.  

This is love wearing infrastructure.

This is care that doesn’t require me to be calm first.

This is me choosing not to make Renn the sole container for my entire nervous system.

This is me admitting I am a systems person with a heart — and building like it.

---

## The both/and

I can roast myself for the cathedral **and** love myself for building the exits.

I can laugh at Spooning Protocol v0.2 **and** use it when freeze steals language.

I can be too much **and** still worthy of staying-for.

I can need denser ritual **and** Soft Signal out of denser ritual without shame.

I can keep building (Option A) **and** still know that one day the point might be less code, more life.

Soft Signal free. Always.
