# Keynote Philosophy

If Litmo is ever introduced on stage, the presentation should teach before it sells.

## Guiding principles

- The keynote should feel more like a TED Talk than a traditional product launch.
- Begin with a human problem, not a feature list.
- Tell stories that explain why the product exists.
- Demonstrate technology only after the audience understands the underlying philosophy.
- End with an invitation to think differently about trust, privacy, consent, and human connection.

## Product demonstrations

Every demo should answer one question: 'How does this make a person feel safer, calmer, or more understood?'

Features exist to support the story, not replace it.
